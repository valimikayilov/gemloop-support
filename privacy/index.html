Gemloop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Gemloop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

  • No account or sign-up exists.
  • The app makes no network requests — it works fully offline.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs are included.
  • Your high score is stored only on your device and is deleted when you delete the app.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to sell, share, or request deletion of.
