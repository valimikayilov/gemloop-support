Gemloop — Support

How to play

Tap anywhere to hop between the inner and outer orbit ring. Avoid the red mines (they flash orange as a warning before they arm), collect the green gems for bonus points, and survive as long as you can — it keeps getting faster.

FAQ

No sound? Gemloop respects the silent switch — flip your phone off silent mode.

Lost my high score? Scores are stored on-device, so deleting the app resets them.
